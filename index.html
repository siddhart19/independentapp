ABOUT

My name is Dimple Rana. I am a mother of two sons Siddharth and Paarth and married for almost a decade to Mr. Sandeep Rana. I am also a passionate PYP educator at Oberoi International school. In my leisure time, I like to dance to the tune of bollywood music and also watch movies. I enjoy travelling with family and go out on adventures with my friends. I hate dishonesty and talking behind the back. I was born in Mumbai however my roots belong to Hamirpur - a beautiful district in Himachal Pradesh, India. I am a proud mumbaikar and can speak and write English, Hindi and Marathi fluently. I enjoy eating the street food like pavbhaji and pani puri a lot. My aim in life is to be someone who would be remembered for the person who I am and hence potray strong beliefs in my values of honesty, love and hardwork. In this page you will find details about my work and my interest.





Profile

I am a PYP educator for past almost a decade now and am a teacher by choice. I love my profession and has gained my expertise in Mumbai PYP schools. Currently I am working at Oberoi International school for past 3 years and have worked across grades from 2 to 5.
